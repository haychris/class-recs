For me, R-coding was the funnest and most useful aspect of the course, giving me a tool I may use in future projects. Overall, I recommend POL 345 lukewarmly; it may well be better than some of the other statistics classes available, but I would look at the one offered in ORF if you want a more quantitative approach.
