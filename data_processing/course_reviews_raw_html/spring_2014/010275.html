Student Comments:
What advice would you give to another student considering taking this course?
A lot depends on which group you choose to work with. In fact, everything depends on it, since each group is, in practice, run as its own separate course. Therefore, in reading these student evaluations, pay careful attention to what group the student is from, since you can essentially regard them as reviews of three different courses. I joined the time team. It was a bit too easygoing - there wasn't much to do most of the time, and there wasn't much guidance on what to do. I personally prefer something a little more tangible and difficult, something that pertains to my ELE major. I would take it again in the future, but after seeing the end-of-the-year presentations from all the groups, I would join another team. (One of the group was designing motor control circuitry, which is something that pertains to my major and is a good practical experience!)
TAKE IT. Perfect as a 5th or 6th course. You will not regret it. Really fun and don't take up a lot of time at all.
