Student Comments:
What advice would you give to another student considering taking this course?
As I have mentioned, the TA's gave me the most help on writing lab reports, so I think it's important to communicate and set up appointments with them a few weeks before the lab report is due. As with all lab courses, it's important to keep a well-written notebook and to make sure you know exactly what you're going to be doing in the lab session in advance.
