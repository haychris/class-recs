Labs, however, are tedious and not very relevant to the course in general, though they are not difficult. I would definitely take it for an STN, and for an STL it isn't bad either.

The labs increase a lot in work as the semester progresses (and can occasionally be confusing) but enhance understanding of the material from lecture.
