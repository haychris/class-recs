Professor Gleason is an outstanding lecturer and this is not your typical English class. For these reasons and for the fascinating history of culture you are privy to through best-selling literature, you should definitely take this course. Be warned that the reading is often frustrating because of how simple and straightforward it is, but Professor Gleason does well in lecture to assuage your frustration and offer different ways to consider and understand these texts both in our moment and in their own moment. The central aim of this course - understanding popular texts as valuable cultural devices - is an important and refreshing way to think about literature.
