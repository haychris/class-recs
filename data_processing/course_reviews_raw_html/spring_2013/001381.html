If Rosen is teaching, take this course! He is a fantastic lecturer and makes a really dry subject genuinely interesting. Problem sets are 20 percent of the grade; they can be tough and tricky, so be prepared to go to McGraw or work with friends. The midterm is 30 percent of the grade, and the final is 50 percent. Both are about 3/4 multiple choice and 1/4 free response. They are quite tricky but not impossible; Rosen likes to test concepts in ways that you haven't seen them before. Case in point: we discussed Pigouvian taxes in class, but on the exam we had to do a subsidy instead. Don't take this class and expect to get an A; only 25 percent of the class gets A grades, and many nasty, hypercompetitive Asians are enrolled, even though they got 5's on their AP micro exam. A very good option as a PDF, as you can learn great material without freaking out about your grade. To sum up: if you can, take Micro with Rosen; you won't regret it.
