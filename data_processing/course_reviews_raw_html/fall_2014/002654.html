I needed another ENG departmental and this fit into my schedule, so at first it was a placeholder. Then when I started asking around, I found that Lee Mitchell is acclaimed by ENG concentrators as one of the best lecturers in the department, so I stuck with it. I am very glad I did. Mitchell has fascinating insights and knows how to present them; I don't think he gave a lecture that I didn't leave chewing on a thought he presented for at least the next couple days. Plus, his method of picking out individual quotes makes a lot of sense for the format of the class and gives an extra push to make sure you go to lecture if only for exam purposes, etc. I was inspired to work harder on my writing for Mitchell than I have been by almost any other professor at this University. He has high standards but knows how to push students toward them in a constructive way. I worked hard for him because he felt I could do better than my first paper and I wanted to prove him right.
I would definitely take this course!
