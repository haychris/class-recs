A good class to take if you are interested in learning about contemporary musicians (Dylan, Paul Simon, Springsteen, David Bowie, The Beatles, U2, etc.) I found the subject material incredibly interesting and often found myself researching independently through wikipedia. The only drawback was that Nigel Smith's lectures were not the greatest. He brought in a lot of guest lecturers, some good and some bad, to break up the monotony. Such great subject matter deserves great lectures and Nigel hasn't reached that point yet. The workload for this class is only concentrated during the midterm and finals period. The rest of the year you don't have to do any readings or assignments. Overall, I'm glad I took the class to have a much better understanding of many of the artists discussed above.
