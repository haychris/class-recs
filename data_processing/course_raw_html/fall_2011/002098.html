Fall 2011-2012
COS 518   No Pass/D/Fail

Advanced Computer Systems

Michael J. Freedman

Survey of operating systems covering: early systems, virtual memory, protection, synchronization, process management, scheduling, input/output, file systems, virtual machines, performance analysis, software engineering, user interfaces, distributed systems, networks, current operating systems, case studies. Survey of research papers from classic literature through comptemporary research. Term paper or project required.


Other Requirements:
Not Open to Freshmen.

Prerequisites and Restrictions:
COS 318 or equivalent.
